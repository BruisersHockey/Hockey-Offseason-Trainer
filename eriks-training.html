Supplements
🧪
What's Worth It
Keep it simple
Whey Protein✅ Essential
Post-workout and before bed. Easiest way to hit protein goals.
Creatine Monohydrate✅ Highly recommended
5g daily, any time. Most researched supplement for strength + power. Safe for his age.
Vitamin D3✅ Recommended
2,000 IU daily. Most athletes are deficient. Supports bone strength and immune health.
Fish Oil (Omega-3)👍 Good addition
1–2g daily. Reduces inflammation, helps joints recover faster.
Pre-workout / Caffeine⚠️ Use carefully
Not necessary at his age. If used, keep it low dose and never within 6 hrs of bedtime — sleep matters more than any supplement.
Magnesium👍 Sleep + recovery
200–400mg before bed. Helps with sleep quality and muscle recovery overnight.
